What To Do In NYC

Hello! My name is Jared Castro and I was born and raised in Brooklyn, NY. I often meet people who are either planning to visit New York City or moving there after college. These people frequently ask me for suggestions on things to do, places to visit, and restaurants to eat at. As a native, I know the best spots around the city and can give the perspective of a real New Yorker. I love giving people recommendations and thus, I decided to make a website with this purpose in mind.
